Chocotorta recipe

An unbelievably easy cake

Ingredients

  • Dulce de Leche or “sweet milk” from Argentina
  • Cream Cheese
  • Creme fraiche or sour cream (optional)
  • Chocolinas or any brand of chocolate cookies
  • One cup of freshly brewed coffee
  • Valrhona dark chocolate (85%) or any quality dark chocolate for garnishing

Steps

  1. Place the cream cheese in a bowl and mix thoroughly with a hand blender. Add in the dulce de leche and continue mixing until you get a light caramel color mixture.
  2. Add sour cream or creme fraiche and mix. Taste as you mix until you get the desired level of sweetness.
  3. Dip the chocolate cookies in freshly brewed coffee and then place them in a dessert tray. While it’s optional, lining the dish with parchment paper makes for less mess and easier cleanup.
  4. Be sure not to dip the chocolate cookies for too long or they will crumble. Once you have the first layer done, spread on dulce de leche mixture and evenly with a spatula
  5. Keep layering your cake until you end on the last layer of the dulce de leche mixture.
  6. For the final layer, melt a few squares of high-quality dark chocolate using the bain marie method. Once melted, spread a thin layer of the chocolate over the last dulce de leche layer.
